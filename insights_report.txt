Employee Attrition Analysis using Python: EDA & Statistical Insights

• Monthly income shows a statistically significant difference between employees who left and those who stayed (T-test, p < 0.05).
• The average employee income differs from the assumed population mean, as indicated by the Z-test.
• Department and employee attrition are statistically dependent, as shown by the Chi-square test.
